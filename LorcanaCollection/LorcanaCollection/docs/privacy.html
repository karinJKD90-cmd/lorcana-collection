Privacy Policy

Lorcana Collection · Last updated: July 1, 2026

Lorcana Collection is a personal card collection tracker for Disney Lorcana TCG. This policy explains what data the app uses and how.

Data stored on your device

Your collection, prices, deck lists, notes, and purchase history are stored locally on your device using SwiftData. This data never leaves your device unless you export it yourself via the CSV backup feature.

Camera

The app uses your camera to scan cards. Card images are sent to the Claude API (Anthropic) for recognition. Images are processed in real time and are not stored by Anthropic beyond the duration of the request. No images are stored by this app.

External services

Lorcana API — card names, set data, and card numbers are fetched from the public Lorcana API to populate the card database. No personal data is sent.

Cardmarket — card identifiers are used to look up market prices. No personal data is sent.

Anthropic Claude API — card images captured during scanning are sent for recognition. See Anthropic's privacy policy.

No accounts, no tracking

The app has no user accounts, no login, no analytics, and no advertising. No personal data is collected or shared with third parties beyond what is described above.

Data deletion

All local data can be deleted by removing the app from your device.
